Crystal Collector!

Welcome to the crystal game! Below you should see 4 crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specified amount of ponts to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Each time the game starts the values of the crystal will change.
